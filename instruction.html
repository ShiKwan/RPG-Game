Super Hero Smash Bros

Game Play Instruction

  1. Turn on your speaker!
  2. Start the game by first selecting your own character (selected character will be highlighted with green border)
  3. Lock in your character by clicking green button "Lock in your hero"
  4. Then select three DIFFERENT opponents to fight against (selected opponents will be highlighted with red border)
  5. Lock in your opponents by clicking red button "Lock in your opponents"
  6. You will be redirect to random fighting background scene!
  7. Select your first opponent and trash him/her by clicking the "Attack" button below your hero
  8. KO! you will then choose your second opponent and then your third.
  9. After beating ALL opponents or lost to your opponents, you can choose to try again by typing 'Y' or pick a different hero and opponents by typing 'R'
Getting annoyed by background music? Turn it off by clicking "Audio" on the top right corner in navigation menu.
Click on "Game" in the top left corner in navigation menu to play!
